Background

I recently graduated from the University of Georgia with a BS in Statistics and BA in Ecology. My undergraduate studies gave me experience with designing/analzing research experiments and exposed me to numerous data science tools/technologies. My final year, I worked as a research assistant on an interdisciplinary project with the UGA Center for the Ecology of Infectious Diseases looking at a possible relationship between hurricanes and the spread of covid-19. This work, along with other various projects I was involved in, inspired me to pursue a career in data science. I enjoy the analytical, problem solving aspect of data, and hope to continue developing my skills and understanding of the field.

Following graduation, I decided to move to Brooklyn as both a way to continue pushing myself and also explore new oppurtunities. Some of my hobbies outside of work include playing ultimate frisbee, chess, and weightlifting.
